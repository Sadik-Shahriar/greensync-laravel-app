The Importance of Recycling in Dhaka

Dhaka, a rapidly growing city, faces significant challenges with waste management. Recycling plays a crucial role in reducing the burden on landfills, conserving precious resources, and mitigating environmental pollution. By embracing recycling, we can create a cleaner, healthier, and more livable Dhaka for all.

The Positive Impact of Your Recycling Efforts

Cleaner Environment

Reduces litter and pollution in our neighborhoods and waterways.

Resource Conservation

Saves valuable natural resources like paper, metals, and plastics.

Energy Efficiency

Lower energy consumption in manufacturing new products from recycled materials.

Economic Benefits

Supports recycling industries and creates green jobs within Bangladesh.

Recycling in Dhaka: How You Can Participate

While formal recycling infrastructure is still developing in Dhaka, individual efforts make a significant difference. You can participate by:

  • Separating Waste at Home: Keep separate bins for paper, plastic, glass, and organic waste.
  • Supporting Informal Waste Collectors: Many individuals and small organizations collect recyclable materials. You can support them by sorting your waste.
  • Finding Local Recycling Centers: Research if there are any local recycling centers or initiatives in your area of Dhaka.
  • Reducing Single-Use Plastics: Opt for reusable bags, bottles, and containers whenever possible.

Basic Recycling Guidelines

Paper & Cardboard

Keep dry and clean. Flatten cardboard boxes. Includes newspapers, magazines, and clean paper.

Plastics

Rinse containers. Check for recycling symbols (types 1 and 2 are often recyclable in more places).

Glass & Metal

Rinse bottles and cans. Separate lids if possible. Aluminum, steel, and glass are valuable recyclables.
